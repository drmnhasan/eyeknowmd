Lesson 1 ‚Äî What Is Pupillary Distance (PD)?

PD is the distance between pupil centers. If PD is wrong, the lenses do not align properly, causing:

  • Headaches
  • Eye strain
  • Distortion or misalignment
  • Unwanted prism
