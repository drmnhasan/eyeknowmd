Lesson 1 ‚Äî What is Pupillary Distance (PD)?

PD is the distance between the centers of your pupils. It tells the lab where to place the optical centers of your lenses.

Why PD matters
  • Headaches and eye strain
  • Distorted vision
  • Unwanted prism
Types of PD

Distance PD is used for far vision. Near PD is used for reading.
